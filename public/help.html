Get on the board

Everyone, once
  1. Sign in at the project URL with your email and password. First time, use the invite or reset link to set your own password on the Set password screen.
  2. The board opens in swimlanes. Use the header toggle to group by Company, Area, Sub-area or Level, and switch Day or Week view. The week header shows real ISO week numbers.
  3. You can view everything but only edit your own company's activities. Admins can edit and move anything.
1

Add an activity

Before the meeting
  1. On your own lane, click an empty day cell, or use the + that appears when you hover a day. The editor opens on that date.
  2. Fill the minimal fields: Description (what), Area / Sub-area / Tier 3 (where), System, Level / Cx stage, Planned start and Duration. Admins also set Company.
  3. Add Constraints (step 2) and use Notes for access, sequence, contacts, risks.
  4. It saves automatically. Drag a card across days to move its start; drag into another lane to reassign trade, area or level.
You can only drag your own uncommitted activities, and never into another company's row.
2

Add constraints

Before the meeting
  1. Open the card and list everything that must be cleared before the work can run: access, permit, prerequisite work, materials, design info, isolation.
  2. Readiness is computed, not declared. A card turns READY only when every constraint is ticked off.
  3. Tick a constraint as make-ready completes; the card flips to ready on its own and the metrics update.
  4. The Constraints log in the left rail lists every open constraint, filterable by company, area, open-only or search, and jumps you to the card.
Make-ready view fades everything ready and spotlights constrained work starting within 7 days. That is your daily picture.
3

Commit the promise

In meeting
  1. Turn Committed on when you are making a reliable promise to deliver the activity as planned. The card shows WILL.
  2. Only commit work that is ready, or will be ready, by its start. PPC measures how many committed promises were kept.
Once committed you cannot reschedule it (start or duration) and cannot un-commit it. You can still update status, actuals, notes and tick constraints. To move a committed date, ask an admin.
4

Witness

In meeting
  1. Turn Witness invite on for any activity the client or a third party must witness. The card shows WIT.
  2. A date and time field appears and is required. The card will not save until you set the slot, so witnessed work always has a booking.
  3. Once your slot is set, an admin exports the witness schedule and calendar invites are sent automatically to the pre-configured groups.
5

Bulk import a batch of activities

Planner or lead, before the session
  1. Open Import and download the template. The Excel version has dropdowns for Area, Sub-area, Tier 3, System and Cx stage; a CSV version is also available.
  2. Columns: Description Company (admins only) Area Sub-area Tier 3 Area System Level Planned start Duration (d) Committed Witness invite Witness date & time Notes. Members import into their own company, so the Company column is ignored.
  3. Required on every row: Description, Area and System. If Witness invite = Yes the row must carry a Witness date & time. Delete the example rows before importing.
  4. Choose Merge (add to what is there) or Override (replace), import, then fix any flagged rows and re-import. Areas, sub-areas and Tier 3s named in the file are created automatically.

Two cadences, same board

Weekly collaborative planning the wall session

  • Build and tidy the 6 week lookahead; add and sequence activities with the trades.
  • Log every constraint and name a make-ready owner and date for each.
  • Commit only the work that is ready for the coming week. Set witness invites for anything the client must attend.
  • Review PPC and open constraints on Reports; agree the make-ready plan before close.

Daily Cx meeting every morning

  • Open Make-ready view; walk only the constrained work starting in the next 7 days.
  • Clear constraints live as make-ready completes; cards flip to ready in front of the room.
  • Update status and actuals on yesterday's committed work; flag anything LATE.
  • Confirm today's and tomorrow's WIT activities have attendees booked.
